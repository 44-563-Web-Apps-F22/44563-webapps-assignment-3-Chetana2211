AMERICAN JAZZ MUSEUM

The museum preserves the history of American jazz music, with exhibits on Parker, Duke Ellington, Louis Armstrong, Ella Fitzgerald and others. Nested within the museum is a fully functioning jazz club, The Blue Room, which holds live performances multiple nights a week.
